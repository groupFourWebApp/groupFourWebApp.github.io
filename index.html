About Us

Comprove is an open source web application providing a space for all indev. The main goal is being free and open place for education, assistance, and collaboration for those who in need in terms of communication development, providing enlightenment of self-discovery and self-worth of every individual whether pwd's, every age, or whatever state in life. We've got you covered through collaborations of the community or self-assessment with curated lists of topic, documentation, and other resources for your needs. With the modern approach of technology, we will utilize this approach through this web app. Remember, A small step or progress is still a progress, be open and free.
